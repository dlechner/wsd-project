Star Wars: The Old Replublic is a MMO developed by Bioware in conjunction with Lucasarts, published by Electronic Arts. It was officially released on December 20, 2011. This website will chronicle the guild Legion of the Republic playing on the Hedarr Soongh PvP Server. The site will focus on our members, news and information pertaining to the SWTOR MMO.
